About FlipZon

At FlipZon, we are committed to providing fast, reliable, and affordable courier services that ensure your packages reach their destination safely and on time. Founded by a passionate group of students at Chitkara University, our journey began with the idea of simplifying parcel delivery and tracking for everyday users like you. We believe that logistics should be simple and easy. With this in mind, we designed FlipZon to offer intuitive features, from booking shipments to tracking them, all through a seamless, user-friendly interface. Our team worked tirelessly to create a platform that you can trust, focusing on the values of speed, transparency, and convenience. Whether you're sending a gift, an important document, or a package, FlipZon ensures that your delivery is in good hands. We aim to make your experience hassle-free with Express Delivery, Package Tracking, and excellent customer support at every step.
